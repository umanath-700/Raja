About Us

Helping Hand is a not-for-profit organisation offering help at home, retirement living and residential care to over 7,000 people in India.

We value independence, choice, freedom and quality of care. We enable our clients, carers and their families to live their lives to the full. We make people feel at home; in our residential homes, in their own home and in their workplace.

Our Mission

To provide innovative and responsive services for older people, which support them to have the best quality of life.

Our Objectives

Our clients’ wellbeing is central to everything we do. We help older people to achieve:

  • Independence and wellbeing – we believe that wellness is a right and is different for each person
  • Dignity of living – we believe everyone deserves exceptional care and attention
  • Quality of life – we believe in maintaining quality of life and sense of purpose
  • Choice – we believe in clients having control over their preferences and decisions
